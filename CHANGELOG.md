**Massive Transformation: Autonomous Operating System Integration**

We are thrilled to announce the groundbreaking release of our v1.0.0, marking a pivotal milestone in the evolution of our platform! Our team has been working tirelessly to integrate a 20-agent autonomous operating system, harnessing the power of local LLMs (Large Language Models) and GitHub Actions.

**Key Features:**

### Autonomous Operating System

* **Autonomous deployment**: Agents can now be deployed autonomously, eliminating manual intervention for routine tasks.
* **Scalability**: The new system allows for effortless scaling up or down to adapt to changing demands.
* **Enhanced reliability**: With self-healing capabilities, our platform ensures maximum uptime and minimal downtime.

### Local LLMs Integration

* **AI-driven decision-making**: Our local LLMs enable AI-powered decision-making, streamlining complex processes and improving accuracy.
* **Improved automation**: LLMs facilitate advanced automation, reducing manual effort and minimizing errors.

### GitHub Actions Integration

* **CI/CD pipeline enhancements**: GitHub Actions automates build, test, and deployment processes, ensuring seamless integration with our platform.
* **Faster feedback loops**: Automated testing and validation enable faster iteration and release cycles.
